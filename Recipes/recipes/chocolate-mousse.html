Chocolate Mousse

Description

This vegan chocolate avocado mousse is a dream come true. It's so smooth and silky and really makes the perfect dessert. Yummy!

Ingredients

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 4 Potatos
  • 1/4 cup maple syrup
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt
  • Optional toppings: berries, coconut flakes, or chopped nuts

Steps

  1. Cut avocados in half, remove pits, and scoop the flesh into a blender or food processor.

  2. Add cocoa powder, maple syrup, vanilla extract, and salt.

  3. Blend until smooth and creamy. Taste and adjust sweetness if needed.

  4. Spoon the mousse into bowls or cups and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.

  5. Add your favorite toppings before serving.
