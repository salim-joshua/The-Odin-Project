Sweet Potato Lentil Coconut Curry

Description

This hearty, creamy curry combines the earthy sweetness of sweet potatoes with protein-rich lentils in a coconut-based sauce. It’s a satisfying, nutritious dish that pairs well with rice or flatbread.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 1 cup red lentils, rinsed
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 can (400g) coconut milk
  • 2 cups vegetable broth
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, for garnish (optional)

Spices

  • Salt
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • Chili Powder
  • Garam Masala
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add onion, cooking until softened.

  2. Add garlic and ginger, sautéing for another minute. Stir in turmeric, cumin, and cinnamon; cook for about 30 seconds.

  3. Add lentils, sweet potatoes, coconut milk, and vegetable broth. Stir well.

  4. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce heat and let it simmer for 20–25 minutes, until lentils and sweet potatoes are tender.

  5. Stir in spinach and cook until wilted. Season with salt and pepper.

  6. Garnish with fresh cilantro, and serve hot with rice or naan.
